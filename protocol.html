Protocol

Before our encounter

Screening

Screening is required for all new clients, and in certain cases when it has been over a year since our last meeting.

Your booking form must be filled out in full to be considered as a serious inquiry.

If you are unable to provide a reference, further screening information will be required. Additional screening methods include: government issued photo ID accompanied by a selfie, and/or employment verification.

Please make an effort to show you are making a serious inquiry! Be a gentleman and introduce yourself, tell me where you found me and give me some information about what you're inquiring about! Sending texts like "Hey" or "You available?" tell me you aren't really serious and looking for a little attention.

Note:
A minimum deposit amount of 30$ when i drive to meet you(outcall) or $25 when coming to me(incall) is required to confirm your request.
Ask for My preferred method for accepting deposits


During our encounter

Our encounter starts the moment you arrive and finishes after we've spent the agreed upon length of time together. If you wish to extend our date, please ask me first. If I am able to extend, then please take care of the remaining balance for the extension promptly

Be gentle! There is no need to be unnecessarily aggressive!

Please do not ask to pay the remaining balance afterwards, it would be awkward if it turns out you didn't have as much as you thought. Please get any and all payment out of the way so nobody has to think about it later


Cancellations


Cancellations

Cancellations made within 72 hours of our arranged booking can have your deposit applied to a future date (within 14 days).

Cancellations made within 48 hours of our arranged booking can have your deposit applied to a future date (within 7 days). Otherwise you will be asked to provide the full valuation amount upon future booking.

In the case of a No Show without communicating a cancellation, I will maintain your deposit and future bookings are unlikely to be reconsidered (except in the case of uncontrollable circumstance).


Locations

  • Incall:

    My Place or in my Car

    • Outcall:

    Venue of your choice (well maintained residence, or the hotel you’re staying)

    Minimum $25 travel fee . Travel fee applied to deposit

    Available by prebooking only

Hygiene & Grooming

Please take extra care to be sanitary for all of our dates, regardless of setting. You will be expected to shower on location upon arrival for incall dates. You should be showered prior to my arrival for outcall dates.

Please be dressed presentably to your level of comfort for all of our dates, regardless of setting.

Rates

Please leave the remaining cash valuation amount in plain sight, in an unsealed envelope or book, before you or I excuse ourselves to freshen up.

In the case of a social date setting, please have the cash valuation amount in an unsealed envelope, discretely packaged in a gift bag or book.

Extensions

When the company is too good to part with, in-date extensions are encouraged!

Short notice extensions can be accommodated up to 6 hours total.

My updated hourly valuation amount must be provided in full upon request for extension. Cash, Transfer, and Crypto are accepted for in-date extensions.

Photography & Filming

I generally do not allow photography and never allow filming in private incall/outcall settings. In public social date settings you may request discrete photos when it is appropriate, and based on our shared genuine enthusiasm.

Courtesy

Dates exceeding 3 hours must include 45 minutes conversation time.

Dates exceeding 24 hours must include unscheduled time, with personal space for me to attend to my everyday needs (such as communicating with loved ones).

Dates exceeding 48 hours must anticipate an additional 7 hour valuation cost per day.

*Dates occurring overnight must allow for a minimum of 5 hours uninterrupted sleep.

Travel

All travel request deposits must include the full cost of transportation.

Travel fees must cover direct round-trip journeys whenever possible.

Please ensure a well maintained accommodation for the duration of my stay.

Travel booking requests are subject to advanced screening measures.

After the encounter

  • Please do not contact me excessively if you do not plan to book me. Holiday greetings and the occasional hello are fine but please do not take advantage and horde my attention

  • If you would like a reference, please let me know before hand. Please do not over use my referral. I will happily give you up to 2 referrals, however if you don't plan to book me again after that then I suggest using your most recently visited companion as a reference to best communicate your most recent behavior
